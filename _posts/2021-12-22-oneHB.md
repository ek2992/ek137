## Target spin symetry 
Due to symmetry, the entropy production will be the same regardless of the order of target spins.

$$M_{012}=\boxed{\boxed{S_{0}} \quad  \boxed{S_{1}} \quad \boxed{S_{2}} \quad S_{3}\quad S_{4}}$$
